## Important caveats

- **No public fundraising** until audit + legal review are complete.
- Code guarantees the **split and transparency**, not the physical purchase of food —
  that relies on receipts, reports, and multisig control with independent reps.
- BTSH is a **donation + governance** token with **no profit mechanics**.
- Legal documents in §7 are **drafts** and are not legal advice.
