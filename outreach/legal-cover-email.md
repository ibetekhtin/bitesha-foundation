not market it as an investment.

**Why you.** We're looking for a firm that covers, together: digital assets / crypto,
DAO governance, US nonprofit / charitable law, and securities. If that's a fit, we'd love
to talk.

**What we need help with (full list attached):**
1. Entity structure — own 501(c)(3) vs. partner charity vs. fiscal sponsor.
2. Charitable-solicitation registration (NY AG Charities Bureau; multi-state).
3. **Securities analysis of BTSH** under the Howey test (our central question).
4. Money-transmission / FinCEN MSB / NY licensing for the USDC→USD step.
5. AML/KYC applicability, tax treatment, DAO operating rules.
6. Review of our token structure, fund flows, and marketing language.

**Materials.** Attached is our documentation package (whitepaper, token model,
tokenomics, charity model, transparency policy, governance, and a structured legal brief

**Important:** we have **not** launched, are **not** soliciting public funds yet, and
intend to complete legal review (and a smart-contract audit) before any launch. We'd
rather adjust the design now than retrofit later — including code changes you might
recommend (e.g. making the token non-transferable, removing DEX liquidity, adding KYC).
